about me

Hey I’m Ruben Elia.
I’m a Communication & Multimedia Design student, currently in my 3rd year.

My work centres around audio-visual design, while my approach to it is driven by collaboration, interactivity, and problem-solving.
To be able to communicate my visions to others, be that in prototypes or finished projects, I developed a diverse set of skills ranging from cinematography all the way to coding.

As a designer, I’m inspired by psychology, the relationship between nature and technology, and the interplay of audio and visuals. This makes me strive to create work that cuts deep into our senses, connecting the human experience with the latest technical developments.
